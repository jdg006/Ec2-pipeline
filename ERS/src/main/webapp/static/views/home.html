Welcome to ERS

What is ERS?

ERS is a website meant to make submitting and approving expense reimbursements easier for both employees and managers.

Request Account

Click below to request an account. Accounts are subject to approval by a superior.

Employees

Employees are able to submit expense reimbursement requests and view their pending and completed reimbursements.

Managers

Managers are able to approve or disapprove expense reimbursements requests and view employee information.
